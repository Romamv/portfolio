Devil May Cry Poster

I made this poster in a challenge created by our class members. Which is to make or redo a video game poster like game covers. Here I chose Devil May Cry 2.

I used several methods: to start I chose to cut off both characters, then I decided to put Path-blur behind the characters (the blur behind), then I put Halftone on both subjects, a gradient map for color, a texture and to finish the title at the bottom.

Igomakopia

I made this poster in the context that Tyler, the creator (here present in the poster) released his new album Chromakopia.

These meager but present similarities with his old project Igor made me want to make a poster by cutting in two the two characters and do as a kind of collage. Then I did as in the old project so path-blur, halftone and a texture.

Hardstone Psycho

I made this poster in the context of listening to Don Toliver's latest album, which is Hardstone Psycho.

Here I take the cover of the album and I did out of frame, I put chains that can remind the chains of a motorcycle but also the biker style. I used again and again Path-blur, Halftone, a gradient map and a texture.

Glowing Aura

I made this poster by chance on a guy who was making a tutorial how to make this kind of poster on photoshop.

I used an application on IOS to make the character in 3D which is MagicPoser that I then imported on photoshop, then I put a circles with blur, aura effect around, grain and texture.

Tame Impala project

This is the first real poster I made on Photoshop/ Illustrator.

Here I decided to take the words of a song by Tame Impala which is The Less I Know the Better, I used images and text to illustrate. Here it is really one or maybe my best project. I used Threshold to make this effect of grainy on the images, then I put a purple color and a paper texture.
